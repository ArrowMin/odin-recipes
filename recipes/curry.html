Japanese Curry

Japanese Curry is a hearty dish that is often made with vegetables, curry sauce, and many spices. It is often served with a chicken or pork cutlet and over rice.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Potato
  • Curry Powder
  • Curry Mix
  • Water
  • Garam Masala

Steps

  1. Cook beef in a large pot
  2. Add carrots, potatoes, garam masala, curry powder, and stir
  3. Add sugar, soy sauce and water, and stir
  4. Add beef, cover and cook for 30 minutes
  5. Take lid off and cook for extra 30 minutes, then server with rice
