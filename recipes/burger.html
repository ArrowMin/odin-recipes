Cheeseburgers

Cheeseburgers are a very popular food in America and can be found in commonly in many restaurants. It is a delicious meal to have alongside fries.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Cheese
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Form patties and place on grill
  2. Cook for 1.5 minutes and flip, place cheese
  3. Cook for another 1.5 minutes
  4. Remove pattiess, toast bread and prepare ingredients to assemble
  5. Spread sauce, build burger
