Shrimp Alfredo

Shrimp Alfredo Pasta is a creamy dish made with alfredo sauce and fettucini noodles, with many spices and topped off with shrimp.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Alfredo Sauce
  • Fettucini Noodles
  • Spinach
  • Red Chili Pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • Bouillon Cube
  • Water

Steps

  1. Chop and prepare all ingredients
  2. Cook pasta in a pot of water
  3. Cook until ready
  4. In a seperate pan cook everything else
  5. Add in noodles into the sauce and serve
